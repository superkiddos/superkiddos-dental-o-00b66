Frequently Asked Questions About Sedation Dentistry

How much does sedation dentistry typically cost at SuperKiddos Dental & Orthodontics in Woodland Hills, and is it covered by insurance?
The cost of sedation dentistry at SuperKiddos Dental & Orthodontics can vary depending on the type of sedation used and the length of the dental procedure. For example, nitrous oxide (laughing gas) usually ranges from $75 to $150 per visit, while deeper sedation options like IV sedation can be $300 or more. Many dental insurance plans in Woodland Hills offer partial coverage for sedation, especially if it’s considered medically necessary for pediatric or special needs patients. Our office staff can provide a detailed estimate before your appointment and help you check your specific insurance benefits.
What is the process for scheduling my child for sedation dentistry at your Woodland Hills office?
To schedule a sedation dentistry appointment at SuperKiddos Dental & Orthodontics, simply call our Woodland Hills office or use our online request form. Before the procedure, we’ll set up a consultation to review your child’s medical history and determine the most appropriate sedation option. On the day of the procedure, our team will provide detailed pre-visit instructions, such as when your child should stop eating or drinking. After the sedation, we’ll monitor your child until they’re ready to go home, and we’ll provide aftercare instructions specific to their needs.
Are your dentists licensed and specially trained to provide sedation for children?
Yes, at SuperKiddos Dental & Orthodontics, all our pediatric dentists and orthodontists are licensed in California and have completed advanced training in sedation dentistry, including courses approved by the American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry. Our team is certified to safely administer various sedation methods and regularly participates in continuing education to stay updated on the latest safety protocols and guidelines.
How long does a typical sedation dentistry appointment take for pediatric patients?
The length of a sedation dentistry appointment at our Woodland Hills office depends on the dental procedure and the type of sedation used. For most routine treatments with nitrous oxide, the entire visit generally takes about 60 to 90 minutes, including preparation and recovery time. If deeper sedation is required for more extensive procedures, appointments may last 2 to 3 hours. We’ll provide you with a clear timeline when scheduling so you can plan accordingly.

At SuperKiddos Dental & Orthodontics, we stand by the quality of our care and prioritize patient safety.
