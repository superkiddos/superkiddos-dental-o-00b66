Frequently Asked Questions About Pediatric Dentistry

How much does a typical pediatric dental cleaning and exam cost at SuperKiddos Dental & Orthodontics in Woodland Hills?
At SuperKiddos Dental & Orthodontics, a standard pediatric dental cleaning and exam typically ranges from $100 to $150, depending on your child's needs and whether X-rays are required. We accept most PPO dental insurance plans, which often cover preventive visits in full. If you're not sure about your coverage, our Woodland Hills front desk team is happy to check your benefits and provide an accurate estimate before your visit.
What is the process for starting orthodontic treatment (like braces or Invisalign) for my child at your Woodland Hills office?
The process begins with a complimentary orthodontic consultation, where our orthodontist will evaluate your child’s teeth and bite using a clinical exam and digital X-rays. We’ll discuss treatment options (such as traditional braces or Invisalign Teen), timeline, and costs. If you decide to proceed, we’ll schedule an appointment for impressions or digital scans. Most patients in Woodland Hills can expect the initial consultation and records appointment to be completed within two weeks, and treatment can usually begin shortly after.
Are your pediatric dentists and orthodontists licensed and experienced in treating children?
Yes, all of our pediatric dentists and orthodontists at SuperKiddos Dental & Orthodontics are fully licensed in California and have specialized training in treating children and teens. Our pediatric dentists have completed additional residency programs in pediatric dentistry, and our orthodontist holds advanced orthodontic certifications. We’re committed to providing gentle, expert care tailored to the needs of children in the Woodland Hills community.
How long does a typical pediatric dental procedure, like a cavity filling, take at your office?
Most pediatric cavity fillings at SuperKiddos Dental & Orthodontics take about 30 to 45 minutes per tooth. We use child-friendly techniques and offer nitrous oxide (laughing gas) when appropriate to help your child feel comfortable. Our Woodland Hills team will walk you and your child through the procedure beforehand and provide aftercare instructions to ensure a smooth recovery.
Do you offer any guarantees or follow-up care for orthodontic treatment?
We stand by the quality of our orthodontic work. At SuperKiddos Dental & Orthodontics, all orthodontic treatments include routine follow-up appointments at our Woodland Hills office to monitor progress and make adjustments as needed. After treatment is completed, we provide a complimentary set of retainers and schedule retention checks to help maintain your child’s smile. If there are any issues with appliances or retainers due to normal wear, we’re here to help with repairs or replacements—just give us a call.
