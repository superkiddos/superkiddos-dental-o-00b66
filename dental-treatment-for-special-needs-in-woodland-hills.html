Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Treatment for Special Needs

What experience does SuperKiddos Dental & Orthodontics have in treating children with special needs in Woodland Hills?
Our team at SuperKiddos Dental & Orthodontics has extensive training and experience in pediatric dentistry for children with a wide range of special needs, including autism, sensory processing disorders, and physical disabilities. Dr. Kim and Dr. Lee are licensed pediatric specialists and regularly participate in continuing education focused on special needs care. We also work closely with families and local therapists in the Woodland Hills area to tailor appointments to each child's unique requirements.
How much does a typical dental visit for a special needs child cost, and do you accept Medi-Cal or other insurance?
The cost of a typical dental visit varies depending on the treatment needed. An initial exam for a special needs child at our Woodland Hills office generally ranges from $120 to $200. We accept most major PPO dental insurance plans, Medi-Cal/Denti-Cal, and can help you verify coverage before your appointment. For uninsured families, we offer flexible payment plans and can provide an itemized estimate after the initial assessment.
What accommodations do you provide for children with sensory sensitivities or anxiety?
We strive to create a calm, supportive environment for every child. Our Woodland Hills office offers private exam rooms, sensory-friendly lighting, noise-cancelling headphones, and weighted blankets. We also schedule longer appointments to allow extra time for acclimation and use visual schedules, social stories, and gentle desensitization techniques. If needed, we can discuss in-office sedation or hospital dentistry options.
What is the typical timeline for starting orthodontic treatment for children with special needs?
After your child's initial consultation and any necessary dental work, we typically schedule orthodontic records (x-rays, photos, impressions) within 1-2 weeks. Once we develop a customized treatment plan, braces or aligners can usually be placed within 2-4 weeks, depending on your child's comfort and readiness. We work at your child's pace and can break treatment into shorter, more manageable visits when needed.
Do you guarantee results for orthodontic treatments, and what happens if my child has difficulty following instructions at home?
While we cannot guarantee specific results due to individual factors and at-home care, our orthodontists closely monitor progress and provide hands-on guidance at every visit. For children who may struggle with oral hygiene or appliance care, we offer additional support, including regular check-ins and in-office cleanings. If challenges arise, we work with parents and local Woodland Hills support resources to adapt the treatment plan and ensure your child receives the best possible outcome.
